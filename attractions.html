Top Attractions in Siquijor

Salagdoong Beach

A famous beach known for its turquoise waters and cliff diving spots.

Cambugahay Falls

A stunning three-tiered waterfall perfect for swimming and rope swings.

San Isidro Labrador Church

One of the oldest churches in Siquijor, showcasing Spanish-era architecture.

Capilay Spring Park

A natural spring-fed pool located in the heart of Siquijor town.

Mount Bandilaan

The highest point in Siquijor, offering panoramic views of the island.

Old Enchanted Balete Tree

A mystical 400-year-old tree with a natural fish spa at its base.
